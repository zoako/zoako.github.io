Set in a vibrant abstract universe, the colors of your ball and the world determine how fast (or slow) you get to go. Modify the track with bright colors as you zip through tunnels planning the perfect route for your next lap.

Mix Colors

Pick up different colors, mixing them into higher-tier colors. Grab both yellow and magenta to level up to red. All three primary paint colors will bring you to the highest tier color, black. You lose color as you paint all the time though, so keeping mixed colors can be a challenge!

Colors are the keys to Speed

Roll over trails or into obstacles of a color you don't have in your ball, and they will slow you down. If they are of a color contained in your ball, they will boost you instead. Higher tier colors amplify both the bonuses and the penalties. Your desired path through the track will change with the color of your ball, each lap different from the last. Obstacles and trails you once avoided can become advantageous.
At the black tier, zip through the level hitting every obstacle you can to try top the leaderboards!
